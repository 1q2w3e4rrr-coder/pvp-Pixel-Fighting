AIZEN Step30 Hotfix - U skill visual anchor fix

Why U still looked like it had a backstep:
- Step29 already locked FighterMain/Node2D world position and blocked body separation.
- The remaining visible backward motion comes from the exported skill1 animation frames themselves:
  idle visible bbox center x ≈ 1722.5
  skill1 frame0-12 visible bbox center x ≈ 1713.0
  difference ≈ 9.5 px left in source canvas
- When P1 faces right, this source-canvas left shift looks like a backward step.
- This is not a move / movePercent / dash / moveToTarget world-position event.

Fix:
- Add get_original_sprite_local_visual_offset().
- In update_frame(), after setting sprite.texture and flip_h, set:
  sprite.position = get_original_sprite_local_visual_offset()
- For skill1 frame_index < 13:
  Sprite2D local offset = Vector2(9.5 * facing, 0)
- All other frames/actions use Vector2.ZERO.

What this changes:
- Only the local visual position of the skill1 sprite for its early frames.
- Does not change ManifestFighter.position.
- Does not change hitboxes.
- Does not change U damage, timing, shine, qi, or endAct/idle events.
- Does not change skill2/skill3, dash, KO, HUD, map, QiBar, HITS, or effect height.
