AIZEN Step39 - disable old KO black sequence and keep custom result menu

Problem:
- The black rectangle still appears after KO.
- Screenshot shows the custom result menu, but the old black KO/Winner sequence remains behind it.

Cause:
- The current scripts still call old FightHud.show_ko(), show_timeover(), show_winner(), show_drawgame() in update_ko_sequence.
- Those call FightStartKOAnimator, whose StartKOSequence is a large pre-rendered texture. Its black-backed frame can remain behind the battle result menu.

Fix:
1. BattlePlaceholder.update_ko_sequence no longer calls:
   fight_hud.show_ko()
   fight_hud.show_timeover()
   fight_hud.show_winner()
   fight_hud.show_drawgame()
2. A custom RoundEndMenu is created and shown instead.
3. The menu subtitle is now:
   Winner：P1
   Winner：P2
   Draw Game
4. While KO/round_end is active, force_stop_legacy_ko_visuals() recursively hides old:
   startko / start_ko / sequence / winner / ko / draw / timeover / bisha / black / dark layers.
5. FightHud is hidden after round end; RoundEndMenu remains visible because it has keep_round_end_ui meta.
